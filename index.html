About

I am an interdisciplinary researcher with more than a decade of experience spanning UNSW Sydney, CSIRO Data61 and IBM Research Africa. My work focuses on distributed systems, cybersecurity frameworks, privacy‑preserving computation and scalable technology solutions for emerging markets. During my PhD at UNSW I developed four blockchain frameworks integrated into CSIRO's Data Clearing House platform, now deployed across more than 200 buildings in the New South Wales Distributed Energy Integration Program with access to over 5 MW of flexible energy capacity. I have authored more than fifteen peer‑reviewed papers in venues such as IEEE Internet of Things Journal, ACM e‑Energy and IEEE ICBC. At IBM Research Africa I co‑architected large‑scale systems including the TradeLens supply chain platform and contributed to patented innovations in distributed systems.

Projects

  • CAESER – Cybersecurity of Consumer Energy Resources (ACM e‑Energy 2024). Multi‑institutional research developing threat models and security frameworks for Australian smart grids and contributing to national energy infrastructure policy recommendations.
  • Data Clearing House (CSIRO). International research program targeting digital transformation of approximately 200 smart buildings (about 5 MW capacity) through cloud and semantic platforms for sustainable urban development and smart cities.
  • HARB – Hypergraph‑based Adaptive Consortium Blockchain (IEEE IoT‑J Q1). Developed novel hypergraph‑based partitioning methodology achieving significant performance improvements in large‑scale IoT networks.
  • PlexiChain – Secure Blockchain‑based Flexibility Aggregator Framework (arXiv 2022). Designed security‑first framework for digital marketplaces using mixed‑methods research combining quantitative performance analysis and qualitative stakeholder assessment.
  • CypherChain – Privacy‑Preserving Data Aggregation Framework (IEEE ICBC 2024). Developed cryptographic protocols enabling privacy‑compliant data sharing for demand response programs.
  • BAILIF – Blockchain Agnostic Interoperability Framework (IEEE ICBC 2023). Led interdisciplinary research team developing interoperability solutions for heterogeneous digital ecosystems.
  • EnergiPay – Off‑chain Payment Channels (ACM e‑Energy 2024). Investigated blockchain‑based payment systems for sustainable energy trading with market analysis, user experience studies and economic modelling.
  • Carbon Certification and Development Initiative (CCDI), UNSW. Conducted systematic analysis of more than fifty academic papers and industry reports on blockchain‑based voluntary carbon standards and secured research funding for sustainable carbon trading systems.
  • TradeLens – Global Supply Chain Blockchain Platform, IBM Blockchain. Contributed to digital transformation impacts on global trade by managing data collection across more than 100 organizations and analyzing efficiency metrics for technology adoption.
